# Features

Aurynk provides a comprehensive suite of tools for Android device management.

## :material-qrcode-scan: Wireless Pairing

Forget about USB cables. Aurynk uses the modern Wireless Debugging protocol introduced in Android 11.

1.  Open Aurynk.
2.  Click **Add Device**.
3.  On your phone, go to **Developer Options > Wireless Debugging > Pair device with QR code**.
4.  Scan the code displayed in Aurynk.
5.  Done!

## :material-monitor-cellphone: Screen Mirroring

Powered by the robust `scrcpy` tool, Aurynk allows low-latency, high-quality screen mirroring.

-   **Control:** Use your mouse and keyboard to control your Android device.
-   **Performance:** High performance and low latency.
-   **Configurable:** Adjust bitrate, resolution, and more in Settings.

## :material-camera: Screenshot Manager

Take high-quality screenshots of your device screen directly from your desktop.

-   One-click capture.
-   Saved directly to your Linux machine.
-   Preview and manage screenshots within the app.

## :material-tune: Device Dashboard

Get a quick overview of your device health and stats.

-   **Battery Level:** Monitor charging status and percentage.
-   **Storage:** See available and used space.
-   **Details:** View Android version, model, and manufacturer info.
