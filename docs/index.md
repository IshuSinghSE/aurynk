## Key Features

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-qrcode-scan: **Wireless Pairing**
    ---
    Connect via QR code - no cables needed! Just scan and go.

-   :material-cellphone-information: **Device Management**
    ---
    View detailed specs, battery life, and storage info at a glance.

-   :material-camera: **Screenshot Capture**
    ---
    Instantly capture and save device screenshots to your PC.

-   :material-monitor-cellphone: **Screen Mirroring**
    ---
    View and control your device screen (powered by scrcpy).

-   :material-palette: **Modern Interface**
    ---
    Beautiful GTK4 design that fits your Linux desktop perfectly.

-   :material-devices: **Multiple Devices**
    ---
    Manage several Android devices simultaneously.
